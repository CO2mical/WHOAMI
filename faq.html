Frequently asked questions


How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?


You have two options to print something from your laptop. The first way is to use mynetpay and log in using your HZ account. Click 'mobility print' and follow the further instructions.

The second way is to Scan the QR-code in the printing room, click on 'mobility printing' and follow the instructions.


How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?


head to the printing room. Once there, swipe your student card to access the printer. Open the printer and align your document with the arrow in the corner. Close the printer, select the subject for your scan, and press start to print the document. Be sure to log out when you're done.


How can you book a project space?


There's a detailed step-by-step guide on the HZ website for this process. First, go to the MyHZ site and log in to continue with the instructions. Once fully logged in, you'll see a page with the "Welcome back" text. Click on "Change my personal menu", then find and add the "Selfservice Portal" to your home menu. Save the changes and return to the home page. From there, click on the Selfservice Portal and select the "reservation" button. Follow the instructions on that page, and you should be able to reserve a room.


How can I buy something on the hz webshop?


  • Go to the HZ website
  • Find whatever you need and add them to your cart.
  • Go to your cart, and fill in your details
  • pay and wait for the items to be delivered to your door. (weirdly there is no pickup option)
